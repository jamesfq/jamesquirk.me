Work and Education

I graduated from Bentonville High School in 2021 in the Top 1% of my class as a National AP Scholar and National Merit Scholar. I was also recognized in the Arkansas Times as a member of the 2021 Arkansas Academic All-Star team.

I'm a member of the Class of 2025 at Dartmouth College majoring in Computer Engineering with a minor in French. On campus, I'm a French Drill Instructor, a TA in the Thayer School of Engineering (ENGS 23), a DCSI fellow, and a member of the Club Ski Team.

I spent the summer of 2022 working full-time as an engineering intern at the US Army Corps of Engineers Cold Regions Research and Engineering Laboratory, where I now work part-time. My research focuses on materials science, specifically with respect to the behavior of aluminum surfaces in cold environments. You can check out my recognition for my research here.

This fall, I was selected by the Dartmouth Center for Social Impact to be the Class of 1982 Upper Valley Community Impact Fellow. Through my fellowship, I will work full-time with the Connecticut River Conservancy this spring to promote extended producer responsibility legislation in the state of New Hampshire.
